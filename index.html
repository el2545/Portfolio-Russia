Core research thesis

My research direction is to build decision-support models that connect physical flows, financial performance, and territorial strategy. The objective is not only to describe urban or supply chain systems, but to make them measurable, comparable, and strategically governable.

  • Urban intelligence: translate traffic and location data into investment decisions.
  • Financial discipline: evaluate projects through ROI, CAPEX, OPEX, TCO, and scenario comparison.
  • Technological sovereignty: design analytical tools that reduce dependence on intuition and strengthen local decision capacity.
Science Measurement, modelling, and reproducibility.
Engineering Tools that can be tested, adapted, and reused.
Strategy Outputs that support allocation of capital and infrastructure.
